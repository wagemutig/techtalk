TALK-TECH is an immersive learning prototype that combines **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and **Extended Reality (XR)** to provide personalized technical English conversation training for vocational education students.

Developed in collaboration with the **Zurich University of Teacher Education (PHZH)** and **afca. ag**, the project leverages the **Xara XR platform** to create realistic customer conversations in which learners explain technical components, functions, and engineering concepts in English.

---

## 🎯 Project Goal

Technical English is a key competency for apprentices in engineering and manufacturing professions. However, large class sizes and limited teacher availability often restrict opportunities for individual speaking practice.

TALK-TECH addresses this challenge by enabling every learner to participate in an AI-driven one-to-one conversation simultaneously, providing:

- Personalized speaking practice
- Increased active speaking time
- Immediate interaction and feedback
- Scalable language training for large classes
- Authentic profession-oriented communication scenarios

---

## 🧠 Learning Scenario

Learners take the role of a technical specialist explaining an engineering assembly to a potential international business partner.

The simulation consists of three phases:

### 1. Briefing
- Introduction to the scenario
- Technical vocabulary preparation
- Instructions provided by an AI mentor

### 2. Technical Conversation
- English-language discussion with an AI customer
- Explanation of components, functions, and technical concepts
- Adaptive questioning based on learner performance

### 3. Reflection
- Analysis of the conversation transcript
- Self-assessment and coaching
- Feedback on vocabulary, grammar, and fluency

---

## ✨ Key Features

### Adaptive AI Dialogue

The conversation dynamically adapts to learner performance using Bloom's taxonomy:

| Level | Focus | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| K1 | Knowledge | Identify and name components |
| K2 | Understanding | Explain functions and relationships |
| K3 | Application | Solve realistic engineering problems |

This adaptive approach ensures learners are challenged according to their individual skill level.

### XR & Cross-Platform Support

The Xara platform supports:

- 🌐 Web browsers
- 📱 Smartphones
- 🥽 Virtual Reality (VR)
- 🪄 Augmented Reality (AR)

This enables flexible deployment in classrooms, remote learning environments, and immersive XR settings.

### Real-World Engineering Context

The simulation is based on authentic engineering drawings and technical assemblies. Learners must apply both technical knowledge and English communication skills in realistic professional situations.

---

## 🎓 Educational Context

The prototype was developed for Swiss vocational education programs, particularly for:

- Polymechanics apprentices
- Automation apprentices
- MEM (Mechanical, Electrical and Metal Industry) professions

The learning objectives align with the **FutureMEM** curriculum and focus on:

- Technical communication in English
- Professional conversation skills
- Engineering component knowledge
- Workplace communication competence
